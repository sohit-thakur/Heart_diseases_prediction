# Heart Disease Data Analysis using Tableau

## Project Overview

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide. Modern lifestyle factors such as unhealthy diets, smoking, lack of physical activity, obesity, and stress significantly increase the risk of cardiovascular diseases.

The goal of this project is to analyze heart disease data using **Tableau**, a powerful data visualization and business intelligence tool. The project transforms raw healthcare data into interactive dashboards that highlight key risk factors and patterns associated with heart disease.

These visual insights can help healthcare professionals, policymakers, and individuals make informed decisions for prevention and early detection.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze heart disease risk factors using data visualization
* Identify patterns related to age, gender, and lifestyle habits
* Detect correlations between medical conditions and heart disease
* Create interactive dashboards to support decision-making
* Promote awareness and preventive healthcare strategies

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains **4500 patient records and 18 attributes** related to demographics, lifestyle habits, and medical conditions.

### Key Attributes

| Category           | Features                                     |
| ------------------ | -------------------------------------------- |
| Demographics       | AgeCategory, Sex, Race                       |
| Lifestyle          | Smoking, AlcoholDrinking, PhysicalActivity   |
| Health Indicators  | BMI, SleepTime, PhysicalHealth, MentalHealth |
| Medical Conditions | Stroke, Diabetes, Asthma, KidneyDisease      |
| Target Variable    | HeartDisease                                 |

## Project Scenarios

### Scenario 1 – Doctor Analysis

A cardiologist analyzes patient lifestyle factors such as BMI, smoking habits, and physical activity to identify high-risk groups and develop awareness programs.

### Scenario 2 – Government Policy Analysis

A government health analyst studies heart disease prevalence across populations to recommend policies like workplace fitness programs and anti-smoking initiatives.

### Scenario 3 – Personal Health Monitoring

Individuals can use simplified dashboards to monitor their health indicators compared to healthy benchmarks and take preventive actions.

## Tableau Dashboards

### 1. Heart Disease Overview Dashboard

Displays overall disease distribution and demographic patterns.

Visualizations include:

* Heart disease cases (Yes vs No)
* Age group analysis
* Gender comparison

---

### 2. Lifestyle Risk Factors Dashboard

Analyzes the impact of lifestyle habits on heart disease risk.

Visualizations include:

* Smoking vs Heart Disease
* Alcohol Consumption vs Heart Disease
* Physical Activity vs Heart Disease
* BMI distribution

---

### 3. Health Condition Correlation Dashboard

Shows relationships between existing health conditions and heart disease.

Visualizations include:

* Diabetes vs Heart Disease
* Stroke vs Heart Disease
* Kidney Disease vs Heart Disease

---

### 4. Patient Health Profile Dashboard

Provides insights into personal health indicators.

Visualizations include:

* BMI vs Age
* Sleep Time vs Heart Disease
* Physical Health vs Mental Health

---

## Key Insights

* Smoking significantly increases heart disease risk
* Higher BMI levels are associated with greater cardiovascular risk
* Physical inactivity correlates with increased disease prevalence
* Patients with diabetes or stroke history show higher risk
